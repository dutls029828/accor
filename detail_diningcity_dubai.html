DISCOVER A WORLD OF FLAVOUR CITY DUBAI

  • A FOOD PARADISE

    There is never a dull moment in Dubai. This vibrant, wonderful, whirlwind of a city is a cultural melting pot, where life is always moving at a hundred miles an hour. It’ll come as no surprise then, that the food scene here is a little bit special. Dubai offers an eclectic collection of culinary options to satisfy any craving imaginable.

  • DINING IN DUBAI

    Through the day, we recommend taking shelter from the heat in one of Dubai’s cafes, coffee shops or delicious brunch spots. Dubai is famous for its bottomless brunches, and it won’t take you long to discover why. As the evening rolls in and the lights begin to sparkle, the city comes alive - and so do its restaurants.

  • BEST BAR IN DUBAI

    After you’ve eaten, you’ll probably find you’re not ready to go home just yet. Lucky then, that Dubai's nightlife is vivacious and lively, with great sports bars, beach bars, jazz bars and dance bars. Or, for a memory that will live with you forever, visit one of Dubai’s rooftop bars and sip on your favourite cocktail while gazing out at the breath-taking scene below.
