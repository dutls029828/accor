    Since you are our valued customer, it is our top priority to provide you with exceptional times and lodging throughout the world. We attach great importance to your satisfacti on with Accor Hotels and your trust in Accor Hotels.     Good to know!

    As you may not know, the hotel you have booked is probably not owned by an Accor SA subsidiary or affiliate. Most of the hotels operating under one of the group's Accor hotel brands are actually franchised or “managed” agreements between the hotel's owner and Accor SA (or one of its affiliates worldwide). This is why, if you stay at one of these hotels, your personal data is processed by Accor Sa and the hotel, each of which is responsible for the processing of the data for its own purposes. To summarize briefly:

    • As Accor SA is responsible for managing the central reservation system, Accor SA may collect data necessary for accommodation arrangements at hotels operated by one of the Accor hotel brands, and may transfer this data to the relevant hotels. Accor SA manages the global information of customers staying at hotels operated by one of the Accor hotel brands. Accor SA, with the help of its subsidiary Pro-Fid SAS, also manages Le Club Accor's fairness program.
    • All hotels process your information to manage their contractual relationship with you (billing, payment, reservation management, etc.), to conduct marketing activities and to comply with their legal obligations.

    Accor SA has transferred all of the principles set out in this Policy to all hotels operating under the Accor Hotels brand and their owners. We will do everything within our capabilities to ensure that all hotels comply with applicable laws and this policy for the protection of personal information.

  3. 3. Accor's ten principles for protecting personal information

    In accordance with the regulations in force, in particular the General Regulations on data protection applicable in Europe, we have established the following ten principles for the processing of your data within Group Accor Hotels.

    1. 1. Legality: We only use personal information in the following cases:
      • Where the consent of the parties has been obtained or
      • Where it is necessary for the performance of the contract by the contracting party ; or
      • Where necessary to comply with a legal obligation or
      • Where it is necessary to protect the vital interests of an individual or
      • Where we have a legitimate interest in using your personal information and this use does not affect your freedoms and interests.
    2. 2. Loyalty: Let us explain how we use the personal information we collect about you
    3. 3. Limitation of purpose and minimization of information: We collect only the personal information we actually need. If the same result can be achieved with less personal information, we will only use that data.
    4. 4. Transparency: We inform you about how we use your information.
    5. 5. We facilitate the exercise of the rights of the parties: access to data, rectification and erasure of data and objection to data processing.
    6. 6. Retention Limitations: We only retain personal information for a limited period of time.
    7. 7. We maintain the security of your personal information, namely its integrity and confidentiality.
    8. 8. If a third party needs to use your personal information, we guarantee the ability to protect your personal information.
    9. 9. If personal data is to be transferred outside the European Union, we guarantee that this transfer is subject to lawful regulations.
    10. 10. If personal information is leaked (lost, stolen, damaged, unavailable, etc.), and if the leak is likely to result in a high risk to the rights and freedoms of individuals, we notify the relevant data protection authorities and the person concerned.
    If you have any questions about the Ten Principles of Accor Data Protection, please contact our Data Privacy Department. See “Your Rights” for more information about our privacy department.
