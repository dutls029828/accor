DISCOVER A WORLD OF FLAVOURS MONACO

  • FROM BRUNCHES

    Enjoying breakfast in Monaco looking out over the sea, booking brunch with friends, dining under the stars… All of these experiences are possible in the principality. You can choose between the chic, fun ambience of a restaurant with a swimming pool and DJ set, a healthy break in a vegetarian restaurant between shopping sessions, or an ex- ceptional dinner offering a fusion of flavours.

  • MONACO TERRACE

    Restaurants in Monaco offer exceptional views across the Mediterranean and the Riviera. Often, the principality's most beautiful terraces are nes- tled in the hillside, as are some of its best hotel restaurants offering breathtaking panoramic views over the sea. As you watch the yachts come and go in the marina, enjoy this wonderful setting for a summer lunch or romantic dinner.

  • DINING IN MONACO

    On the Côte d'Azur, Monaco is a byword for pres- tige and gastronomy. From the Rock to the sea, the best restaurants in Monaco offer a uniquely special experience. Foodies flock here to sample the refined and inventive cuisine concocted by chefs inspired by the local Mediterranean produce that is so readily available to them.
