HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

ACCOUNT

MANAGE MY ACCOUNT & PROBLEMS WITH MY ACCOUNT
  1. How do I update my personal information in my account?
    To update your personal information, please proceed as follows:
    1. 1. Click «Login» to access your account
      • on the all.accor.com website
      • in the all.accor.com mobile application
    2. 2. Enter your email address or ALL - Accor Live Limitless card number and password
    3. 3. Click on « My Profile » section
    4. 4. Go to « My Details »
    5. 5. After making any changes you want to make, click « Save »
  2. I want to cancel my membership. How do I cancel my membership?
    You may cancel your ALL - Accor Live Limitless account at any time.
    1. 1. Go to « Support Page » on all.accor.com
    2. 2. In the top right corner, click «Login» to enter your email address or ALL card number and password.
    3. 3. « ALL » Choose a subject for your request
    4. 4. « Other base oils » Choose a reason
    5. 5. If desired, state the reason for termination in « Comments »
    6. 6. Complete your request by clicking « Send Request »
  3. I can't log in to my account. What should I do?
    • Make sure you entered your email address correctly.
    • Make sure the email address you entered is the same one you used when setting up your account.
    • Make sure the password you enter is the same as the password you used to set up your account (if your password is different, click «Forgotten your password?» )
    • Make sure the Accor mobile app or website is not under maintenance. At the top of the «Contact Us» page, check for downtime-related messages.
  4. How do I find my ALL - Accor Live Limitless card number?
    How to find your ALL - Accor Live Limitless card number
    1. 1. Access your « Account » via all.accor.com
    2. 2. Enter your email address and password
    3. 3. Click « My Loyalty Area and Subscription Account » then « My Loyalty Card »
    4. 4. Card number displayed
    * If you have not yet received or lost your card number, you can redeem your card in digital form on the all.accor.com app via your phone or tablet.
  5. How do I combine my cards into one account?
    If you have multiple ALL - Accor Live Limitless cards registered under the same name, you can request to combine them into one account.
    1. 1. Go to « Support Page » on all.accor.com
    2. 2. In the top right corner, click «Login» to enter your email address or ALL card number and password.
    3. 3. After logging in, go back to the center of the web page and fill out the online contact web form.
    4. 4. « ALL » Choose a subject for your request.
    5. 5. « Request to combine multiple cards » Choose a reason.
    6. 6. In "Comments", specify how many cards you want to incorporate, specify the cards you want to keep and the email addresses associated with those cards.
    7. 7. Complete your request by clicking « Send Request »
  6. My membership card is about to expire. Is the ALL - Accor Live Limitless loyalty card still valid?
    • Loyalty or membership cards are separate.
    • Membership cards have an expiration date.
    • Loyalty cards are valid for life (Silver, Gold and Platinum tier levels have an expiration date).
    • Therefore, if your membership card expires, your ALL - Accor Live Limitless loyalty card will remain valid.

  9. How do I register a bank card to my account?
    Registering one or more bank cards to your account will make future bookings on the website or Accor mobile app even easier. To register a bank card to your account, please proceed as follows:
    1. 1. Log in to your account.
      • on the website all.accor.com
      • In the Accor mobile app
      • In the Accor tablet app
    2. 2. Access your account by clicking on the icon at the top of the screen or by clicking « My Account » in the sub-menu that appears when you hover your mouse over it.
    3. 3. Enter your bank details in the « Bank Card » section.
      • Cardholder name
      • Card number
      • Expiration period
      • Security Code (3 digits on the back of the card, 4 digits for American Express) This information is used to verify that the card is a valid card and is not stored.
    4. 4. Click « Save » to confirm.

  11. How do I change the banking information in my account?
    1. 1. Log in your account.
      • on the website all.accor.com
      • In the Accor mobile app
      • In the Accor tablet app
    2. 2. Access your account by clicking on the icon at the top of the screen or by clicking « My Account » in the sub-menu that appears when you hover your mouse over it.
    3. 3. Select the desired card from the list in the « Bank cards » section and click « Edit ». You can change the name of the card or cardholder.
    4. 4. Click « Save » to confirm.
  12. How do I activate my account and view the terms and conditions of the ALL - Accor Live Limitless program?
    To activate your account and complete your membership, access all.accor.com
    1. 1. Under « ALL, FIRST-TIME LOGIN » click « PASSWORD/ACTIVATION »
    2. 2. Enter your name and email address
    3. 3. Click « OK »
    * You will receive an automatic welcome email. To complete your membership, click « Complete Membership » and then follow the instructions to activate your account and view the terms and conditions for using the program.
  13. Why should I create and customize an account?
    • Once you have entered your details, they will automatically appear, saving you time when making a new reservation.
    • You can access your reservation records through your account. You can also change your current reservation at any time.
    • Just enter your favorite brands, destinations and preferences and we'll get recommendations on the mobile app and website for hotels that suit you.
    • You can manage your newsletter subscriptions in your account so you never miss out on special promotions.
    • You can also access your Customer Loyalty Program account and enjoy all the benefits offered by the program at your fingertips.
  14. How do I delete the bank card registered to my account?
    1. 1. Log in your account.
      • on the website all.accor.com
      • In the Accor mobile app
      • In the Accor tablet app
    2. 2. Access your account by clicking on the icon at the top of the screen or by clicking « My Account » in the sub-menu that appears when you hover your mouse over it. Bank details related to the selected card will be permanently deleted immediately.
  15. Why am I receiving newsletters in other languages?

    ALL is always doing its best to provide the best accommodation and use experience with services tailored to customer needs. For this reason, ALL sends newsletters with the latest information so that customers can enjoy all the benefits of ALL to the fullest. Marketing promotional emails or newsletters may be in a different language than the language used when booking your stay, as we provide you with products that are most relevant to your country of residence.For reservation-related emails, you can choose your preferred language in your member account. If you no longer wish to receive newsletters in the language of your country of residence, please log in to ALL and unsubscribe from your member account. ALL is always ready to help you, so if you have any other questions, feel free to ask. We hope to see you on your next trip with ALL..
