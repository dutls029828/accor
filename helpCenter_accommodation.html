  1. What payment methods do the hotel accept?

    Accepted payment methods are country. Depends on the hotel, and the price of the reservation. To find out if the hotel you plan to stay in accepts your payment method, contact the hotel directly. You can find contact information on the hotel's description page.

  2. Why do I need a debit/credit card when making a reservation?
    Bank details are required to confirm your online reservation, unless special conditions are stated at the time of booking. Bank details are required to guarantee your reservation. The hotel will charge the indicated amount within the hotel for the length of stay. For some reservations, prepayment is required (see terms and conditions of the reservation rate) and will be paid directly by the hotel for all or part of the stay. The reservation system is secure, so you can provide information with confidence. If you do not wish to guarantee your reservation by providing your banking information online, you may do so through the Reservations Department of Accor Hotels.
    Enter the card number (leave blanks), expiration date (the card used must be valid for the time of stay) and card security code in the relevant fields (secure input using SSL password) unless special conditions or special rates apply. We require your bank details to secure reservations using a credit or postpaid card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Diners Club, etc.). Upon arrival at the hotel, guests must have the credit/gift card used to guarantee or prepay the reservation. * For more information, please refer to the Payment Methods in Article 16 « Payment » of the Terms and Conditions of Sale.
  3. Are there any special programs or regulations for children?

    Children under 18 years of age stay free of charge when sharing a room with an adult. This offer applies to all room rates. Additional charges may apply when booking packages with other inclusions. Children's menus are available at all Fairmont properties. Children under 5 years old enjoy free children's menu, children 6-12 years old receive 50% discount. Babysitting is available at all Fairmont facilities. Some hotels also have children's programs and activities under the supervision of a representative. For specific details, see Guest Services in the hotel profile.

  4. How do I search and book hotels?
    How to search for a hotel
    • If you know the exact name of the city you are traveling to, you can enter the city name in « Find Hotel ». This way, you can quickly select your travel destination and hotels available for the dates you specify. You can find all hotels in full without specifying the dates of your stay. When you select a hotel, you will receive booking options.
    • You can find hotels close to your address or traveler location on a city map. You can also search using the map search tool.
    • All hotels are displayed regardless of availability, but rooms available for reservation are displayed.
    • Always a special rate for the day at the selected hotel.
    How to book a hotel
    1. 1. Search for hotels at your destination that fit your selection criteria
    2. 2. Select one from our list of suggested hotels to view rates
    3. 3. Choose from a wide range of suggested rates to meet your needs, taking into account your specific selling conditions.
    4. 4. Add the necessary ancillary services, then check the details of the reservation, the total price and the corresponding terms of sale
    5. 5. Fill in your contact information and then create an account for faster access to our online booking service.
    6. 6. Enter your bank details to secure your reservation
    7. 7. Read and accept the Terms of Sale
    8. 8. Reservation confirm
    9. 9. You will receive an immediate acknowledgment of your reservation receipt.
    10. 10. The website and mobile services immediately send an email outlining the contractual benefits, services booked, price, terms of sale for the selected rate, date of reservation, and place of stay, acknowledging receipt of the customer's reservation.
  5. My company has a special rate contract with a Fairmont hotel. Can I make a special rate reservation online?

    Yes, a special promo code is required and must be entered when checking availability. To check if your code has already been confirmed or if you would like to request a code assignment, please contact our corporate customer service team by email. Proof of eligibility is required when checking in at this rate.

  6. How do I reserve a room for people with reduced mobility?

    Look for this icon on the description page of your preferred hotel (this icon indicates that the hotel has rooms that give priority to guests with reduced mobility). To find out which facilities are available, check the hotel's « Services » section, description of the room types available for reservation at the hotel, and/or contact the hotel directly. You may also contact the hotel directly to make a reservation and discuss your specific needs. You can find the hotel's direct contact information on the hotel's description page.

  7. What services does the hotel provide for people with disabilities?

    All of our locations have rooms for guests with reduced mobility. Please contact the hotel directly to request a full list of available facilities and services and to confirm availability.

  8. Do you pay directly when you provide your credit/debit card number or at the hotel?

    A credit/debit card can be used to guarantee the reservation of a room for the night. Except as otherwise stated in the Rate Description and Terms of Sale, there is no charge and you pay for your stay at the hotel. Rates cannot be changed or cancelled, provided that the immediate rate is prepaid.

Please read the terms of sale for the proposed rates carefully so that you can choose the best option for you.

  2. I need to cancel my reservation. Can I get a refund?
    If your plans change, you may cancel your reservation if cancellation is permitted under the terms of sale and cancellation. In other words, for all or part of the stay, you will be charged for any non-cancelled reservations in accordance with the terms of sale of the rate booked. Depending on the rate, cancellation of the reservation may be subject to terms and conditions. Look for the terms and conditions of sale related to the rate in the section «Cancellation Deadline». There are no refunds for prepaid reservations. For more information, please see our Terms of Sale.
  3. If I made a reservation online, can I change or cancel my reservation by contacting the hotel directly?

    You can change or cancel your reservation online by contacting the hotel directly. However, as some fares are subject to change in price or cancellation under certain conditions, you should first consult the terms of sale for the reserved fare. Please check these terms and conditions to see if you can change your reservation by contacting the hotel.

  4. How can I check if my reservation has been confirmed?

    If you do not receive your reservation email or reservation number, please contact the hotel to confirm that your reservation has been confirmed. If you have an 8-character reservation number, go to the Manage My Bookings section and enter your reservation number, last name and arrival date to access your reservation.

  5. How do I use « Online Check-in / Express Check-out »?
    « Online check-in / fast check-out » allows ALL., Accor Live Limitless members to save time and make it even easier.
    • Before arriving at the hotel, you will receive an email to complete your online check-in. Information can be entered in advance using the ALL card. Upon arrival at the hotel, your keys are ready and you receive a personalized welcome.
    • Check-out time is further shortened. No waiting, just return the key, and you'll receive your bill via email.
  6. When can I enter my room?

    You will find this information in the Conditions of Sale for the rate in the « Check-in time » section when making your reservation. Check-out time varies by hotel. Some hotels offer early check-in so you can get to your room early. Reservations can be made online through the website. Simply select this service before confirming your reservation to use it. This service is a payment service and is subject to the hotel's rate conditions.

  7. I was charged multiple times for the same reservation. What should I do?

    Contact a customer service representative who will assist you with your request by verifying all necessary points.

  8. What is the Best Price Guarantee?

    If you book an Accor Group hotel on one of our websites and within 24 hours find that the same offer is offered at a lower rate on another website for the same hotel, we will give you an additional discount of 10%.

    You made a reservation through our reservations department or website
    • Book a hotel on one of the Accor Group websites with the lowest rate and receive a booking confirmation number.
    • If you find a lower price for the same product on another website within the next 24 hours (same hotel, same date, same room type, same sale conditions, etc.), fill out the online form on the website and attach the required supporting documents to get the lowest price . Apply for coverage.
    • After verification of the submitted information, an additional 10% discount will be applied to the lowest confirmed rate.
    • You can stay at the lowest rate guaranteed for your room.
  9. Can I request an extension of room occupancy on the day of departure?

    Please refer to the « Check-out time » section of the terms of sale of the reserved rate to find out what time the room must be vacated. Some hotels offer late check-out to extend your stay. Reservations can be made online through the website. Simply select this service before confirming your reservation to use it.This service is a payment service and is subject to the hotel's rate conditions.

    • If you have an account, log in and check your reservation in the «Manage My Booking» section, then resend your email.
    • If you do not have an account, please go to Login to resend your booking confirmation email.
  12. Until when will the hotel reserve my room?

    In case of late arrivals, we can guarantee your reservation with your credit/debit card number. This commitment guarantees reservations for rooms after the hours stipulated in the brand's policies. Likewise, it means that the hotel promises to hold this reservation until noon the next day. If you do not arrive at the hotel and do not cancel your reservation, the first night will be charged.

    For information about your hotel's reception opening hours, please refer to the « Hotel Accessibility » section of « Services and Leisure Activities » on the description page of the hotel you are trying to book.

  1. If a dispute that arises during your stay is not resolved amicably, I would like to contact an arbitrator.

    Customers should first contact their customer service representative or hotel to resolve the dispute amicably. If the response is negative or if there is no reply within 60 days of filing a claim, the customer may contact the arbitrator. We are travel and tourism mediators for Accor Group hotels, hotels managed by AccorHotels Group, or franchised hotels that have decided to have resources for mediators for travel and tourism. The customer may contact the arbitrator within 12 months of filing the first claim.
