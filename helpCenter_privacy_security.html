Frequently Asked Questions

SECURITY

PRIVACY & SECURITY
  1. How do I change my personal information?
    You have the right to rectify incorrect personal information or to fill in missing information. You can log in to the website and change your personal information on the 'Your Information' page. If you do not have a personal account or for any inquiries regarding changes to your personal information, you may contact us by sending an email to data.privacy@accor.com.
  2. How do I delete my personal information?

    You have the right to erasure of your personal information. You can delete some of your personal information directly from the 'Your Information' page by logging into the website. If you do not have an account or have questions about deleting your personal data, you can contact us.

  3. How do I cancel my newsletter subscription?
    You may unsubscribe from the newsletter by logging into the website and accessing the 'Newsletter' page or by clicking on the unsubscribe link at the bottom of each newsletter. Alternatively, you can send an email to data.privacy@accor.com with any questions regarding your newsletter subscription.
  4. How do I protect my privacy and access to my account?
    We strongly recommend that you take the following security measures:
    1. 1. Prohibition of Disclosure of That Information to Others
    2. 2. Change your password regularly
    3. 3. Set a different password for every app you use
    4. 4. Click on the « My Newsletter » section, check the sender's email address to campaign. Note: The email address used by the newsletter is the domain name@accor-mail.com . If in doubt, please contact our customer service team.
    * Accor respects the regulations of the French Data Protection Authority (CNIL). Please refer to the General Terms and Conditions of the ALL - Accor Live Limitless program for details.
  5. What should I do if I ask for details about the privacy policy or if I have other requests?
    You have the right to be informed clearly, concisely and transparently about how the Accor Group handles your information through simple procedures. All matters related to your personal information handled by the Accor Group are described in detail in the 'Customer Information Protection Charter' at the bottom of the homepage. Alternatively, you may send an email to data.privacy@accor.com for any inquiries regarding the use of your personal information.
  6. How can I access my personal information?
    You have the right to request a copy of your data processed by the Accor Group. You may request access to your personal information by sending an email to data.privacy@accor.com.
  7. Is it safe to register a bank card for my account?

    At Accor, the protection of your banking information is our top priority. Card registration is very secure. Your banking details are transmitted to Accor via a secure connection (https page) and stored in a secure digital vault. The banking information stored in the safe is only used when it is sent to your bank to authorize your card or payment. The Accor system will ask you to enter a security code (3 digit number on the back of your card) to confirm use of your card. Accor will also contact your bank to verify that your card is not suspended. If your card is suspended, the transaction will be declined.

  8. Where can I read Accor’s ‘Customer Data Protection Charter’?

    You can read the 'Customer Information Protection Charter' by clicking the 'Personal Information' link at the bottom of the website.

  9. How is personal information and account access protected?

    Whether intentional or accidental, the Accor group has taken technical and administrative measures to protect your data from destruction, loss, distortion, misuse and unauthorized access to, alteration or disclosure of data. To this end, the Accor Group takes technical and administrative measures to ensure that the confidentiality, integrity, usability and resilience of our personal information handling systems and services are maintained on an ongoing basis. Regarding the ID and password required for login, we recommend that you keep the following for security reasons.

    • 1. Please do not disclose personal information to third parties.
    • 2. Change your password periodically.
    • 3. Use different passwords for each service you use.
    • 4. Check the sender address of your marketing mail. Most Accor groups use addresses of the form @accor-mail.com (your domain name).
    * If you have any doubts in this regard, you may notify us by sending an email to data.privacy@accor.com.
  10. What if I do not wish to provide my personal information to the hotels of the Accor group?
    For the purpose of improving service quality and increasing customer convenience in using Accor Group's various facilities, your accommodation information, preference, satisfaction level, or membership, if necessary, in accordance with the 'Customer Information Protection Charter (can be found at the bottom of the website)' information may be provided to the hotels of the Accor group. If you do not want your personal data to be provided to the hotels of the Accor group, you can request to do so at any time. You may request that we stop providing you with your personal information by sending an email to data.privacy@accor.com.
  11. As a California resident, how fo I exercise my "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" right?
    The California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 ("CCPA") gives Californians the right to opt-out of the "sale" of their personal information, which is very broad in its definition of "sale". Strictly speaking, the Accor group does not 'sell' your personal data. Nevertheless, given that the Accor Group's business activity is construed as a 'sale' under the CCPA, California-based customers may exercise their right to 'request to stop selling personal information'. If you wish to make the above request: You may send an email to data.privacy@accor.com.
  12. Is my banking informatoin stored in my personal account protected?

    The Accor group takes the protection of your banking information very seriously. Your bank card numbers are stored with a very high level of security. Your banking information has been transmitted to the Accor group via a secure connection and is stored in a secure digital vault. Your banking information is always kept in a secure deposit box except when it is passed on to your bank for valid card verification or payment verification purposes. To ensure the security of your card use, the Accor group asks you to enter a security code (three digits on the back of your bank card) each time a transaction occurs. Also, if your card usage is declined, we will contact your bank to confirm that the card usage has been suspended according to your will.

  13. How should I deliver instructions on the use of personal information after death?
    You have the right to provide the Accor group with instructions regarding the subsequent use of your personal data. You may exercise this right by sending an email to data.privacy@accor.com.
  14. How do I exercise the right to portability of personal information?
    You have the right to download, reuse or transfer the personal data you have provided to the Accor group to other data processors. This right applies based on your prior consent or mutual agreement regarding the handling of personal information. You may request portability of your personal information by sending an email to data.privacy@accor.com.
  15. What are cookies on Accor websites?

    If you would like more information about our use of cookies or your cookie settings, you can view our Cookie Policy by clicking on the 'Cookies' link at the bottom of our website.

  16. Can I book a thalassotherapy treatment?

    To learn about and book all the treatments available at your destination, visit the Thalassa Sea & Spa website. You can also contact our reservations department on 00 33 (0) 1 72 95 00 33 for more information about accommodations
