  2. 2. Rates
    • Accommodation service

      The accommodation service rates are per apartment and per night, and include utilities (water, electricity, heating). The rates shall vary according to the booking date, the arrival date, length of the stay, type of Apartment (studio, two bedrooms, etc.), the number of people occupying the Apartment (within the maximum limit allowed per Apartment) and the type of rate (Flexible, Semi-Flexible, Non-Flexible).

    • ADAGIO offers three types of rates:
      • Flexible rates: these rates require a booking deposit (see Article 3 below) with full payment of the balance on arrival to the Aparthotel (see Article 4 below), and allows full cancellation without charge subject to conditions (see Article 5 below).
      • Semi-Flexible rates: these rates require full payment in advance when booking (see Articles 3 and 4 below), and allows full cancellation without charge subject to conditions (see Article 5 below).
      • Semi-Flexible rates: these rates require full payment in advance when booking (see Articles 3 and 4 below), and allows full cancellation without charge subject to conditions (see Article 5 below).
      • Non-Flexible rates: these rates require full payment in advance when booking (see Article 4 below), and does not allow cancellation (see Article 6 below).
    • ADAGIO shall apply a tiered pricing system in relation to the length of stay:
      • For stays of four nights or more, the price of the accommodation service is reduced from the first night in all Aparthotels and across all periods (except for special conditions that are specified at the time of booking).
      • All bookings, regardless of their origin, are payable in the local currency of the Aparthotel.
      • In the event of a discrepancy between the number of peoples indicated in the reservation and the number of people on the day of arrival, a fixed-rate supplement of €15 per additional person and per night will be applied on arrival, the accommodation of the extra individuals being conditional to the capacity of the booked Apartment(s).
      • Additional products and services are not included in the price of the accommodation service.
    • Housekeeping:
      • Prices for stays of eight nights or more include a daily housekeeping service that covers cleaning of the Apartment (except the kitchen and dishes), a change of bed linen and towels and cleaning at the end of the stay.
      • Prices for stays shorter than eight nights do not include a housekeeping service. This service can be requested directly at the reception of the Aparthotel and charged as an additional cost.
    • Breakfast service:
      • Some rates include a breakfast service. In this case, breakfast is included for the entire duration of the stay and for all the people included in the booking.
      • For all other rates, breakfast is not included and is payable at the location. Breakfast is free for children up to the day before their third birthday, subject to a minimum Breakfast charge.
    • Additional services:
      • Additional services (parking, on-site shop, late check-in etc.) will be charged as an additional cost unless expressly included in an offer.
    • Promotional offers:
      • Promotional offers are subject to the special conditions communicated with the offer. Promotional offers may not be combined.
    • Rate changes:
      • ADAGIO reserves the right to adjust rates in the event of legislative and/or regulative changes likely to cause a fluctuation in prices (particularly the modification or creation of taxes). Any modification or creation of new legal or regulatory fees imposed by the competent authorities will automatically be reflected in the price indicated on the billing date.
    • Tourist tax:
      • Tourist tax is not included in the rates. The tourist tax amount shall be determined per person and per day and shall vary according to local regulations and current residence classifications. This is payable either on site or when booking, depending on the circumstances.
    • “Starting from” prices:
      • The prices indicated in any communication material and on the website in close connection with the reference “STARTING FROM” are indicative of offers made for specific lengths of stay, time periods and cities.
    • Baby policy:
      • Accomodation is free for all children under three (3) years old, provided that this has been indicated at the time of booking. Baby kits (chair and cot) are available to customers through booking at reception and while supplies last.
  3. 3. Guarantees
    Accommodation service:

    All bookings must be approved using a valid credit card number for the duration of the stay.

    For reservations with Flexible rates, a deposit will be taken from the credit card when booking:
    • For all stays between one and three nights: a bank pre-authorisation (PLBS) corresponding to the price of the first night.
    • For all stays between four and nine nights: a bank pre-authorisation (PLBS) corresponding to the price of the first two nights.
    • For all stays between ten and 27 nights: a deposit that is four times the amount for the first night.
    • For all stays lasting 28 nights or more: a deposit that is eight times the amount for the first night.
    • For reservations with Semi-Flexible rates, the deposit taken from the credit card shall correspond to the total amount paid for the booking.

    The guarantee may be used by ADAGIO in the event of non-payment by the occupant of the amount due to ADAGIO for the accommodation service and/or additional services used on site (breakfast, telephone, parking, etc.).

    A seasonal rental contract shall be concluded between the Aparthotel and the occupant for stays within the French territory lasting more than 28 days. The occupant must provide the following documentation: Three most recent payslips and/or last tax assessment notice, an up-to-date proof of address, bank statement, a copy of a valid identity document and a bank guarantee in the form of a credit card number under the name of the booking party that is valid until the end of the stay. Neither the booking nor the extended stay will be confirmed unless all of the documentation is available at the time of signing the accommodation contract.

    Under no circumstances can the customer make the rented premises their primary residence for any reason. The Customer agrees to have an effective primary residence throughout the rental period. A joint inventory will be drawn up between ADAGIO and the Customer on arrival and departure from the Apartment.

  4. 4. Payment of booking
    Payment period: For reservations with Flexible rates, payment shall vary depending on the length of stay and the brand concerned:
    • For all stays between one and 14 nights: the balance of the full stay must be paid on arrival, less the deposit.
    • For all stays between 15 and 27 nights: payment for the first 15 nights is due on arrival, less the deposit. The balance must be settled before the end of the first fortnight.
    • For all stays lasting 28 nights or more: payment for the first 15 nights is due on arrival, less the deposit. Each fortnight must be paid in advance during the stay.
    Payment methods:
    • For payments via the ADAGIO website: payment by credit card (Mastercard, Visa, AMEX) for all Aparthotels and PayPal (UK), Diners Club and Accor Business Account for certain Aparthotels.
    • For payments via the ALL website: payment by credit card (Mastercard, Visa, AMEX) for all Aparthotels and PayPal (UK), Diners Club, Accor Business Account, Mister Cash, iDeal, SOFORT Banking, JCB, Alipay, UnionPay card, bank transfer and WeChat Pay for certain Aparthotels.
    • For payments via an external partner website: please refer to the possible payment methods on their website.
    • For payments at an Aparthotel: by cash (in compliance with the regulations in force on the date of payment), credit card (Mastercard, Visa, AMEX) or bank transfer.
    Late payment:

    In the event of non-compliance with the above-mentioned payment times, late payment interest may be applied by ADAGIO. This late payment interest shall be due the day after the invoice due date at a rate that is three times the legal interest rate in force on the invoice is due, applied to the total outstanding amount on said invoice, inclusive of tax. Payments made via the ADAGIO website and ALL websites are PCI-DSS certified.

  5. 5. Changing / cancelling a booking
    Conditions for total cancellation of the booking: For reservations with Flexible rates, the time limit for free booking cancellation shall depend on the total duration of the stay initially booked. Free cancellation:
    • For all stays between one and three nights: up to 6:00 p.m. on the day of arrival.
    • For all stays between four and nine nights: up to 12 noon the day before arrival.
    • For all stays between ten and 27 nights: up to 12 noon, 3 (three) days before arrival.
    • For all stays lasting 28 nights or more: up to 12 noon, five (5) days before arrival.
    Beyond these time periods and in the event that the occupant fails to arrive on the scheduled date, ADAGIO reserves the right, except where required by specific local regulations, to apply the following cancellation fees:
    • A deduction to the value of 1 night at the booked rate, for all stays between one and three nights.
    • A deduction to the value of two nights at the booked rate, for all stays between four and nine nights.
    • Non-repayment of accommodation deposit for stays of 10 nights or more.
    For reservations with Semi-Flexible rates, the time limit for free booking cancellation shall depend on the total duration of the stay initially booked. Free cancellation:
    • For all stays between one and three nights: up to 12 noon, 3 (three) days before arrival.
    • For all stays between one and three nights: up to 12 noon, 3 (three) days before arrival.
    • For all stays between ten and 27 nights: up to 12 noon, 7 (seven) days before arrival.
    Conditions for changing a booking:

    Any changes to the date, length of stay, type of Apartment or stay location in the booking initially confirmed by ADAGIO shall be considered a partial cancellation of the booking. If the partial cancellation of a booking corresponds to a change in the length of stay, this is likely to result in a change in the rate, given that ADAGIO's tiered pricing system is in relation to the length of a stay. If the length of stay is shorter than that initially booked, the rate change will be retroactive up to the day of arrival. Subject to availability and at ADAGIO's discretion, the length of the stay may also be changed with no obligation to stay in the same Apartment or for the same price.

    For reservations with Flexible rates, in addition to a change of rate, any partial cancellation of a booking may result in payment of the following cancellation fees:
    1. 1. In the event of partial cancellation of a booking before the arrival date, no charge will be applied:
      • For all stays between one and three nights: up to 6:00 p.m. on the day of arrival.
      • For all stays between four and nine nights: up to 12 noon the day before arrival.
      • For all stays between ten and 27 nights: up to 12 noon, 3 (three) days before arrival.
      • For all stays lasting 28 nights or more: up to 12 noon, five (5) days before arrival.
    2. 2. In the event of partial cancellation of a booking after the arrival date (early departure), no charge will be applied:
      • For all stays between one and nine nights: up to 12 noon the day of early departure
      • For all stays between ten and 27 nights: up to 12 noon, 3 (three) days prior early departure.
      • For all stays lasting 28 nights or more: up to 12 noon, 5 (five) days early departure.
    3. 3. Beyond these time periods, ADAGIO reserves the right to apply the following cancellation fees and is entitled to charge:
      • One night for all stays between one and nine nights.
      • The nights cancelled (up to a limit of four nights) for all stays between ten and 27 nights.
      • The nights cancelled (up to a limit of eight nights) for all stays lasting 28 nights or more.
    For reservations with Semi-Flexible rates, in addition to a change of rate, any partial cancellation of a booking may result in payment of the following cancellation fees:
    1. 1. In the event of partial cancellation of a booking before the arrival date, no charge will be applied:
      • For all stays between one and three nights: up to 12 noon, 3 (three) days before.
      • For all stays between four and nine nights: up to 12 noon, 3 (three) days before.
      • For all stays between ten and 27 nights: up to 12 noon, 7 (seven) days before arrival.
    2. 2. In the event of partial cancellation of a booking after the arrival date (early departure), ADAGIO shall apply a cancellation fee that is equivalent to the full stay and therefore shall not refund the deposit.
    Notification: To ensure that all changes (full or partial cancellations and extensions) are accepted, notification should be sent:
    • Directly by email to the Aparthotels or via the booking system (booking cancellations can be carried out directly on www.adagio-city.com in "My bookings" under "My account") where the booking has been made directly with ADAGIO.
    • To the service provider used when booking the Apartment for all other cases.
