DISCOVER A WORLD OF FLAVOUR CITY JEDDAH

  • NEW-WORLD DISH

    A historic crossroads of pilgrims and traders and the traditional gateway to Mecca, the port city of Jeddah is fast becoming a popular destination with tourists seeking culturally immersive experiences. Today, this thriving city offers up a multitude of attractions, from ancient walkways to waterborne activities and a unique culinary scene serving everything from sensational street food, to gastro- nomic, fine dining restaurants.

  • ALL-YOU-CAN-EAT

    Start the day soaking up the friendly chatter in one of Saudi’s famous cafes. Sit outside and watch the city bustle by or join in one of the traditional board games that are played in coffee shops across the city. Big breakfasts, generous brunches and open buffets are all on offer here, so come and recharge before heading back out to explore the many heri- tage-steeped treasures.

  • FLAVOUR SENSATION

    International food is also firmly on the menu, with Indian, Asian and Italian restaurants in abundance. Lavish afternoon tea is a popular past time in Jedd- ah, but make sure you save space for later because there’s no shortage of luxury dining experiences. Dine by candlelight in one of the restaurants on the vibrant Jeddah Waterfront or bring your brood for a lively family meal.
